OUR STORY


Sante Restaurant is housed an historic five-story building in Old Montreal, constructed in 1886 by architect Victor Roy.


You will enjoy our restaurant with:

  1. A variety of delicious food.
  2. Wine from all over the world.
  3. Various activities.

For a great evening with friends, communicate directly with the restaurant, we can offer group menus.
